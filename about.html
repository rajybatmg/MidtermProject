Our Story

Founded in the heart of Korea, Kyochon Clone began with one mission: to serve the crispiest, juiciest fried chicken in the world. Every bite is a blend of tradition, flavor, and crunch.

We value quality ingredients, ethical sourcing, and the joy that comes from sharing a meal with loved ones.

  • Over 500 locations globally
